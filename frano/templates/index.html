Frano strips away all the bullshit and shows you what you really want to know about your portfolio.
We strip down your portfolio to a few basic statistics: How is your money allocated? How much did it cost you? How much are you making? What is the time weighted return? We keep your portfolio anonymous yes accessible.

  • No logins, No passwords We do not ask you to sign up, simply create a portfolio and bookmark the URL, you can always go back to it when you need.
  • Simple Setup You will need to know the cost basis of positions in your portfolio and how much cash you have. Thats it!!!
  • Easy Sharing We provide you a special URL you can use to show your portfolio to others, without them being able to change anything about it.
  • How Do I Start? Use the form at the top of the page to create your portfolio.
